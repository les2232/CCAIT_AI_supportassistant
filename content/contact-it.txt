CONTACTING CCA IT SUPPORT

How to contact CCA IT Support:
- CCA IT Helpdesk:
  - Phone: 303-360-4900, Monday–Friday, 8 a.m.–5 p.m.
  - After-hours/weekend support: 1-888-800-9198
  - Email: HelpdeskTickets.CCA@ccaurora.edu

How to submit a support ticket:
- Email HelpdeskTickets.CCA@ccaurora.edu to submit a support ticket.
- Include as much detail as possible in your email.
- Attach screenshots and error messages if you have them.
- Include your student ID number and contact information in the email.

When to contact IT:
- Contact CCA IT Support if you cannot resolve your issue using the steps provided.
- Use the CCA IT Helpdesk contact options above for urgent, ongoing, or after-hours technical issues.
