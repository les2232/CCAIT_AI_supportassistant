IT RESOURCES

CCA IT Help Portal:
- The CCA IT Help Portal is a self-service resource for IT-related topics.
- Access it by scanning the IT Help QR code or searching for IT Help on the CCA website.
- The portal provides helpful guidance on common IT issues.

The Hub:
- The Hub offers technology assistance, tutoring, library research, and printing services.
- The Hub is available at both CentreTech and Lowry campuses.
- To find The Hub, search "HUB" from the CCA college home page or scan the Hub QR code.
- CentreTech location: Classroom Building 107.
- CentreTech phone: 303-360-4736.

When to use The Hub:
- Visit The Hub for in-person technology assistance on either campus.
- The Hub also supports tutoring, library research, and printing from personal devices.
