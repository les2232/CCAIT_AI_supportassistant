Technology Help & Support at Community College of Aurora (CCA)

This document summarizes how students can get help with technology, devices, software, and learning tools at CCA.

--------------------------------------------------
IT SUPPORT CONTACTS

IT Helpdesk
- Phone: 303-360-4900
- Hours: Monday–Friday, 8:00am–5:00pm
- After-hours support available 24/7

MyCourses / D2L (Canvas) Helpdesk
- Phone: 1-888-800-9198
- Available 24/7

--------------------------------------------------
WHERE TO GET TECH HELP

Daily Laptop Lending Program
What this is:
- Students can check out a laptop for use on campus during the day.

What to know:
- Laptops must be returned at the end of the day.
- An agreement form must be signed each time.

Where to go for more:
- For semester-long laptop loans, visit Student Success.

--------------------------------------------------
Loan Headphones

What this is:
- Noise-canceling and regular headphones are available.

Where to go:
- Hub Front Desk.

--------------------------------------------------
Software Availability (Adobe, SolidWorks, Coding Software)

What this is:
- Mac computers have Adobe Creative Suite.
- One desktop computer has SolidWorks.
- Student laptops include coding software.

What to know:
- Hub staff do not help install software on student devices.
- An exception is made for a printing application.

Where to go for more:
- Contact IT for troubleshooting or additional software needs.

--------------------------------------------------
Loan Calculators

What this is:
- Students can check out a TI-84 graphing calculator.

What to know:
- Calculators are loaned for the semester with an agreement.

Where to go for more:
- Hub Front Desk.
- A free online graphing calculator is available at Desmos.com.

--------------------------------------------------
Printing Help / Hub Printers

What this is:
- Students can print from Hub printers.

What to know:
- Student-owned laptops must connect to the CCAStudents Wi‑Fi network.

Where to go for help:
- Hub Front Desk for printing assistance.

--------------------------------------------------
Logging into Hub Computers & Laptops

What this is:
- Assistance with logging into Hub computers or loaner laptops.

What to know:
- Students use their student number (S#).
- Password is the one created for MyCCA.

Where to go for help:
- For password resets, contact IT at 303‑360‑4900.

--------------------------------------------------
Office 365 App Support

What this is:
- Help with Office 365 applications (Outlook, Word, Excel, PowerPoint).

Where to go:
- Contact IT at 303‑360‑4900.
- After-hours support available at 1‑888‑800‑9198.

--------------------------------------------------
Navigate (College Portal)

What this is:
- Help signing in to Navigate.

Where to go:
- Hub Front Desk.
- A Navigate how‑to video is available.

--------------------------------------------------
MyCourses / D2L (Canvas) Support

What this is:
- Support for accessing and using MyCourses (Canvas).

What to know:
- Students should register in the D2L Student Orientation Course.
- Enrollment steps:
  1. Log into D2L through MyCCA
  2. Scroll to the bottom
  3. Select Self‑Registration Courses
  4. Click Discovery Tool – Register for D2L
  5. Under available courses, search for D2L

Where to go for help:
- D2L Helpdesk is in the MyCourses portal.
- Brightspace provides official D2L resources.
- 24/7 D2L Support Phone: 1‑888‑800‑9198

--------------------------------------------------
Assistive Technology

What this is:
- Accessibility and assistive technology resources.

Where to go:
- Assistive Technology Guide
- Office of Disability and Equity Website
