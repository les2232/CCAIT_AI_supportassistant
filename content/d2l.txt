D2L (Brightspace)

What this is:
- D2L, also called Brightspace, is the learning management system used by the Community College of Aurora.
- Students use D2L to access course content and online learning resources.

What students use it for:
- Viewing course materials.
- Submitting assignments.
- Participating in online course activities.
- Accessing course-related learning resources.

How to access:
- Access D2L through the Community College of Aurora student systems using your student account credentials.
- Sign in with your CCA student account to open your courses and course materials.

Where to get help:
- If you are unable to access D2L or your course materials, contact CCA IT Support for assistance.
