ZOOM SUPPORT

What this helps with:
- Use this guide if you need help joining a Zoom class or fixing common Zoom problems at the Community College of Aurora.
- Zoom may be used for online, blended, or remote class meetings.

Joining Zoom classes:
- Open the Zoom link provided by your instructor or in your course materials.
- Follow the prompts to join the meeting.
- If the meeting does not open right away, reopen the link and join the Zoom meeting again.

App or browser:
- Zoom may open in the Zoom app or in a web browser, depending on your device and settings.
- If the app does not launch, look for the option to join from your browser if it is available.
- If you are using the app, make sure it is fully open before trying the class link again.

Audio or video problems:
- If your Zoom audio is not working, check that your microphone, speakers, and camera are connected and turned on.
- Make sure Zoom is using the correct microphone and speaker if your device has more than one option.
- If audio or video is not working, leave the meeting and rejoin.
- You can also restart the app or your device and try again.

Sign-in problems:
- If Zoom is asking you to sign in and you cannot continue, start again and make sure you are using the correct account information if sign-in is required.
- If the meeting link is not working, check your course instructions and try opening the link again from the original source.
- If you still cannot join because of a login or access problem, contact CCA IT Support.

When to contact OBL:
- Contact Online and Blended Learning (OBL) for help with course-related Zoom support, online learning tools, or questions about how Zoom is being used in a class.

When to contact IT for Zoom help:
- Contact CCA IT Support for Zoom login issues, access problems, device-related trouble, or ongoing technical issues that prevent you from joining or using Zoom.
