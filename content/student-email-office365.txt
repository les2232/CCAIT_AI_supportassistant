  STUDENT EMAIL / OFFICE 365 TRAINING

  What this is:

  - A student-facing collection of Office 365 training tutorials.
  - Training covers Microsoft 365 tools such as Outlook, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Teams, OneDrive, and Office Online.
  - The resources are primarily video tutorials designed for both beginners and users who want to improve their skills.

  What students use it for:

  - Learning how to use Outlook and other Office 365 tools more effectively.
  - Creating, editing, formatting, and sharing documents in Word.
  - Working with spreadsheets, calculations, charts, and data in Excel.
  - Building and improving presentations in PowerPoint.
  - Communicating and collaborating in Microsoft Teams.
  - Storing, organizing, syncing, and sharing files in OneDrive.
  - Using Office applications online from a web browser across different devices.

  How students access the training:

  - Some tutorials are housed in LinkedIn Learning.
  - Students need to activate their LinkedIn Learning account before using those tutorials.
  - LinkedIn Learning access uses a CCA email account.
  - Students sign in using their S# and MyCCA password when prompted.
  - Access to the training requires a LinkedIn Learning account if the tutorial is hosted there.

  Things to know:

  - The training is video-based.
  - Tutorials include both quick tips and full essential training courses.
  - Quick tip videos are short and focused on common tasks or questions.
  - Full courses provide broader instruction on core features and workflows.
  - Training is grouped by product area, including Teams, Office 365, OneDrive, Word, Excel, and PowerPoint.

  Where to get help / When to contact IT:

  - No specific IT contact or escalation details were included in the pasted content.
  - IT support is available for account or access issues.
