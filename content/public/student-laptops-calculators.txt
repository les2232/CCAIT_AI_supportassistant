STUDENT LAPTOPS & CALCULATORS

What this is:
- Information about semester laptop borrowing for currently enrolled CCA students and calculator checkout for CCA math students.
- These resources are provided to support coursework and campus technology needs.

What students use it for:
- Borrowing a laptop for the entire semester if they do not have one.
- Using shared laptops and desktop computers in the Hub.
- Borrowing a graphing calculator for math courses.

Laptop Loan Program (Semester Loan):

Who is eligible:
- Currently enrolled Community College of Aurora students can ask about loaner laptop availability.

What is provided:
- Loaner laptop availability and included equipment can vary.
- Ask Student Success what is currently available.

How to get a semester laptop:
- Start with Student Success in the Student Center.
- Ask about the current laptop checkout process and availability.
- Bring your student ID and current course schedule if they are requested.
- If a student does not have a student ID:
  - Visit the Welcome Desk in the Administration Building at CentreTech Campus to obtain one.

Additional details:
- Exact inventory, checkout steps, renewal options, and eligibility details can change.
- Confirm the current process with Student Success before relying on laptop availability.

Calculator Loans:

What is available:
- Graphing calculators for CCA math students.

How to get a calculator:
- Visit the Hub front desk at CentreTech Campus.
- Provide your S# to check out a calculator.
- Calculators are loaned for the entire semester.

Hub Technology:

Hub laptops:
- Loaner laptops can be found in the Student Center. Ask staff about current availability and checkout requirements.

Desktop computers:
- Adobe Creative Cloud products are installed on CCA Macs. Access is limited to graphic design students or students whose class requires Adobe.
- If your class requires Adobe, you should be able to sign in on a CCA Mac.
- SolidWorks is installed on Innovation Lab computers and CAST 132 computers.

Where to get help / When to contact support:
- For questions about the Laptop Loan Program, contact Student Advocacy:
  studentadvocacy.cca@ccaurora.edu
- For general technology assistance, students may ask the Hub.
