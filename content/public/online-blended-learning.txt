ONLINE AND BLENDED LEARNING (OBL)

What this is:
- The Online and Blended Learning (OBL) team at the Community College of Aurora supports online and blended instruction.
- OBL helps students and faculty navigate online learning tools, instructional support questions, and accessibility-related technology guidance.

Location:
- Go to Fine Arts Building, Room F103 if you need to find OBL in person.

What students use it for:
- Participating in online and blended courses at CCA.
- Using online learning tools such as D2L (MyCourses) and Zoom when those tools are part of a course.
- Finding the right support path for online learning tools used in class.

Services provided:

Learning technology support:
- D2L (Learning Management System)
- Zoom (web conferencing)
- YuJa (video creation, storage, and curation)
- Simple Syllabus
- Turnitin (plagiarism checking)
- Video recording room

Quality assurance support:
- 15-week course template
- Healthy Course Checklist
- Design Quality Matters (QM) rubric

Access, equity, and advocacy support:
- D2L accessibility checker
- Equatio (digital math support)
- Read&Write (literacy support tool)
- Accessible Office templates
- Panorama and accessibility training

Training requirements:
- Faculty and instructors may be asked to complete training before teaching online or blended courses.
- Training expectations depend on role and teaching assignment.
- If training questions affect course delivery or faculty support, OBL can help direct you to the right next step.

Design and support access:
- Contact the OBL design team if you need help with online course design, course structure, or related instructional support.
- Use OBL contact information if you are not sure whether your question belongs with OBL or IT.
- Contact OBL for instructional support questions related to online or blended course design.

Where to get help / When to contact OBL or IT:
- Contact onlinelearning.cca@ccaurora.edu for help with online course design, course structure, or instructional support.
- Contact onlinelearning.cca@ccaurora.edu for online learning design support or instructional questions.
- Contact the CCA IT Helpdesk for technical issues with course systems, account access, or login problems.
- CCA IT Helpdesk:
  - Phone: 303-360-4900, Monday–Friday, 8 a.m.–5 p.m.
  - After-hours/weekend support: 1-888-800-9198
  - Email: HelpdeskTickets.CCA@ccaurora.edu
