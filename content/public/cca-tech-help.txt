Technology Help & Support at Community College of Aurora (CCA)

This guide helps students find the right place to get help with technology, devices, software, and campus support resources at CCA.
It is also the best starting point when you are not sure which IT support option fits your issue.
Use this guide when you need help finding the right IT support option and do not know which support resource to use first.

--------------------------------------------------
IT SUPPORT CONTACTS

IT Helpdesk
- Phone: 303-360-4900, Monday–Friday, 8 a.m.–5 p.m.
- After-hours/weekend support: 1-888-800-9198
- Email: HelpdeskTickets.CCA@ccaurora.edu

--------------------------------------------------
WHERE TO GET TECH HELP

Finding the right IT support option:
- Use this section if you need help finding the right IT support option for a student technology issue.
- Start here when you are not sure which support resource, office, or help desk fits your question.
- If you still are not sure where to go, ask the Hub Front Desk for help choosing the right support option.

Daily Laptop Lending Program
What this is:
- Students can check out a laptop for short-term use on campus during the day.

What to know:
- Laptops must be returned at the end of the day.
- An agreement form must be signed each time.

Where to go for more:
- For semester-long laptop loans, visit Student Success.

--------------------------------------------------
Loan Headphones

What this is:
- Noise-canceling and regular headphones are available.

Where to go:
- Hub Front Desk.

--------------------------------------------------
Software Availability (Adobe, SolidWorks, Coding Software)

What this is:
- Some campus computers include software students may need for coursework.
- Examples include Adobe Creative Suite, SolidWorks, and coding software.

What to know:
- Access may depend on the computer or location you are using.
- Use this section to find where programs are available on campus.

Where to go for more:
- Contact the CCA IT Helpdesk for troubleshooting or additional software needs.

--------------------------------------------------
Loan Calculators

What this is:
- Students can check out a TI-84 graphing calculator.

What to know:
- Calculators are loaned for the semester with an agreement.

Where to go for more:
- Hub Front Desk.
- A free online graphing calculator is available at Desmos.com.
