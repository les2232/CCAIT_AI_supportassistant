  STUDENT EMAIL / OFFICE 365 TRAINING

  What this is:

  - A student-facing collection of Office 365 training tutorials.
  - Training covers Microsoft 365 topics such as Outlook, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Teams, OneDrive, and Office Online.
  - The resources are primarily video tutorials designed for both beginners and users who want to improve their skills.

  What students use it for:

  - Learning how to use Outlook and other Office 365 tools more effectively when they are available to your account.
  - Creating, editing, formatting, and sharing documents in Word.
  - Working with spreadsheets, calculations, charts, and data in Excel.
  - Building and improving presentations in PowerPoint.
  - Learning about Microsoft Teams collaboration features that may be available to your CCA school account.
  - Learning about OneDrive file storage, organization, sync, and sharing features when those services are available to your account.
  - Learning about Office applications online from a web browser when web apps are available to your account.

  Using your school account with Microsoft 365:

  - Sign in with your CCA school account and check which Microsoft 365 services are currently available to you.
  - Look for services such as Outlook, OneDrive, Teams, or Office Online after you sign in.
  - Use only the Microsoft 365 services that are currently available to your CCA school account.
  - Contact the CCA IT Helpdesk if OneDrive, Teams, Office Online, or another Microsoft 365 app or service is missing, unavailable, or not working.
  - Access depends on current student licensing and service availability.

  How students access the training:

  - Open the training resource and check whether the tutorial is hosted in LinkedIn Learning.
  - Activate your LinkedIn Learning account before using a tutorial that is hosted there.
  - Sign in with your CCA email account, S#, and MyCCA password when prompted for LinkedIn Learning access.

  Things to know:

  - The training is video-based.
  - Tutorials include both quick tips and full essential training courses.
  - Quick tip videos are short and focused on common tasks or questions.
  - Full courses provide broader instruction on core features and workflows.
  - Training is grouped by product area, including Teams, Office 365, OneDrive, Word, Excel, and PowerPoint.

  Where to get help / When to contact IT:

  - Contact the CCA IT Helpdesk if a Microsoft 365 app or service is missing, unavailable, or not working with your CCA school account.
