D2L / Desire2Learn

You are in the right place. These steps can help you get to your course materials.

What this is:
- D2L/Desire2Learn is the learning management system used by the Community College of Aurora.
- Students use D2L to access course content and online learning resources.
- Students access D2L/Desire2Learn courses by logging in to MyCCA and clicking the Desire2Learn/D2L link.

What this helps with:
- Use this guide if you need to access D2L, Brightspace, course materials, assignments, or online course resources.
- Use this guide if you are new to D2L and need a simple starting point.

What students use it for:
- Viewing course materials.
- Submitting assignments.
- Participating in online course activities.
- Accessing course-related learning resources.
- Checking whether your computer and browser are ready for online courses.

Online course readiness:
1. Make sure you have reliable internet access for online courses.
2. Open https://cca.desire2learn.com/d2l/systemCheck and run the D2L/Desire2Learn system check before online courses.
3. If you have never taken an online class, meet with a Pathways Advisor before enrolling, especially if you are a new student.

Try this first:
1. Log in to MyCCA with your CCA student account.
2. Click the Desire2Learn/D2L link.
3. Open your course list.
4. Select the course you need.
5. Open the course materials, assignment, or online learning resource you need for class.

How to access:
1. Log in to MyCCA with your CCA student account if you cannot access your D2L course materials.
2. Click the Desire2Learn/D2L link.
3. Select the course or course material you need.
4. For online courses, open https://cca.desire2learn.com/d2l/systemCheck and run the D2L/Desire2Learn system check.

If that did not work:
1. Try signing in again and enter your CCA student account information carefully.
2. Check whether the issue is with signing in, seeing your course, opening course content, or submitting an assignment.
3. Use the D2L Troubleshooting guide if D2L does not load, your course is missing, or an assignment upload is not working.

Still need help?:
- If you are unable to access D2L or your course materials, contact the CCA IT Helpdesk.

What to include when asking for help:
- Your student ID (S#) or CCA username if the issue affects sign-in.
- Whether you can sign in to D2L.
- Whether the issue affects one course or multiple courses.
- The course, assignment, or page you are trying to open.
- The exact error message or page behavior you see.
- The device and browser you are using.
- What steps you already tried.
