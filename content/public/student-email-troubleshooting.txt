STUDENT EMAIL TROUBLESHOOTING

You are in the right place. These steps can help you get back into your student email.

What this helps with:
- Use this guide if you are having trouble with your Community College of Aurora student email account.
- It covers common problems with Outlook, Microsoft 365 email access, password prompts, and sign-in verification.

Try this first:
1. Sign in with your correct CCA student email account and current password.
2. Confirm that your Microsoft Authenticator or MFA enrollment is complete before trying to sign in to student email.
3. Approve the Microsoft Authenticator or MFA sign-in prompt and finish the verification step before returning to Outlook.
4. Try Outlook on the web to check whether your student email account opens there.
5. If Outlook on the web works but the app does not, close Outlook completely and open it again.
6. Sign in to the Outlook app again with your CCA student email account.

Cannot access student email:
1. Make sure you are using your CCA student email account and the correct password.
2. If sign-in does not work, start again and confirm that your account information is correct.
3. Approve the Microsoft Authenticator or MFA prompt and complete the verification step if the sign-in page asks for it.
4. If you still cannot access student email, try Outlook on the web before contacting CCA IT Support.

Outlook keeps asking for my password:
1. Enter your current CCA account password carefully and try again.
2. If the password prompt keeps coming back, close Outlook and reopen it.
3. If you still cannot get into your email, the issue may be related to your account, password, or sign-in verification.

Outlook is not loading or email won't open:
1. Close Outlook completely and open it again.
2. If your email will not open, try using Outlook on the web to check whether the issue is limited to the app.
3. If the web version works but the Outlook app does not, the problem may be limited to the app on your device.
4. If Outlook still is not loading and your student email still is not working, continue with the additional checks below.

MFA or verification loop:
- If your student email keeps sending you back to a verification screen or you cannot finish sign-in, start again and make sure your account information is correct.
- Approve the Microsoft Authenticator prompt or complete the required verification step without closing the sign-in window if student email sends you back to verification.
- If your Microsoft Authenticator or MFA enrollment is incomplete or you cannot finish setup, complete enrollment before signing in again, or contact the CCA IT Helpdesk.
- If you changed phones, lost access to your verification method, or are not receiving the verification prompt or code, contact the CCA IT Helpdesk.
- Verification problems may prevent access even when your password is correct.

Outlook app or web access:
- Use this section if you are deciding whether to use Outlook on the web or the Outlook app for student email.
- Student email may be available through Outlook in Microsoft 365 or through the Outlook app, depending on how you are signing in.
- If you are not sure whether to use the app or the web version, start with Outlook on the web and confirm that your student email opens there.
- If the Outlook app is not working, try accessing your student email through the web version instead.
- If web access works but the app does not, the problem may be limited to the app on your device.

If that did not work:
- Check whether the issue is a password problem, an MFA or verification problem, an Outlook app problem, or a web access problem.
- If the password is not working, use the CCA Login and Password Help guide.
- If MFA or verification is blocking sign-in, use the MFA and Account Security guide.

Still need help?:
- Contact the CCA IT Helpdesk if you cannot access your student email, Outlook keeps asking for your password, or verification problems prevent you from signing in.

When to contact IT for student email help:
- Contact the CCA IT Helpdesk if you cannot access your student email, Outlook keeps asking for your password, or verification problems prevent you from signing in.

What to include when asking for help:
- Your student ID (S#) or CCA username.
- Whether the issue happens in Outlook on the web, the Outlook app, or both.
- Whether MFA or verification appears during sign-in.
- The exact error message or prompt you see.
- The device and browser or app you are using.
- What steps you already tried.
- A screenshot if the page shows an error or warning.
