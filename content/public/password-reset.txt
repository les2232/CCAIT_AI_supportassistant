PASSWORD RESET

You are in the right place. These steps can help you get back into your CCA account.

What this helps with:
- Use this guide if you need help logging in to myCCA, signing in on a CCA computer, or resetting your CCA password.
- Your myCCA password is the same as your password for CCA computers and some other CCA services.

When to use it:
- You forgot your password.
- Your password is no longer working.
- Your account is locked.

Try this first:
1. Go to the myCCA login screen at ccaurora.edu.
2. Click Menu, then select myCCA.
3. Sign in with your S# and password if you know your current password.
4. If you forgot your password, follow the prompts on the myCCA login screen to reset your password.
5. After your password is set, try signing in again with your S# and password.

How to reset your password:
1. Go to the myCCA login screen at ccaurora.edu.
2. Follow the prompts on the myCCA login screen to reset your password.
3. Use your S# and password to log in once your password is set.
4. If login still does not work, contact the CCA IT Helpdesk.

How to log in on a CCA computer:
1. Use your S# and password to log in on a CCA computer.
2. Remember that your PC password is the same as your myCCA password.
3. If the CCA computer does not accept the password, try signing in to myCCA to check whether the same password works there.

If that did not work:
1. Type your S# and password again slowly.
2. Check that Caps Lock is not turned on by mistake.
3. If the sign-in page says your account is locked, wait a few minutes, then try signing in again carefully.
4. If your password is correct but another sign-in check is failing, use the MFA and Account Security guide.

If your account is locked:
- Account lockouts are usually caused by too many incorrect password attempts.
- Wait a few minutes, then try signing in again carefully.
- If you still cannot access your account, contact the CCA IT Helpdesk for help unlocking it.

If another sign-in check is failing:
- If your password is correct but another sign-in check is failing, use the MFA and Account Security guide for approval-prompt, authenticator-app, changed-phone, or verification-code issues.

Still need help?:
- Contact the CCA IT Helpdesk if the password reset process does not work or your account stays locked.

What to include when asking for help:
- Your student ID (S#) or CCA username.
- Whether you are trying to sign in to myCCA, a CCA computer, or another CCA service.
- The exact error message or sign-in prompt you see.
- Whether the issue is a forgotten password, a password that stopped working, an account lockout, or an MFA issue.
- What steps you already tried.
- A screenshot if the page shows an error or warning.
