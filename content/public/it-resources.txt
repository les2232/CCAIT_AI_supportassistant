IT RESOURCES

CCA IT Help Portal:
- The CCA IT Help Portal is a self-service resource for IT-related topics.
- Access it by scanning the IT Help QR code or searching for IT Help on the CCA website.
- The portal provides helpful guidance on common IT issues.

The Hub:
- The CentreTech Hub is in the Classroom Building, Room 107.
- The Hub provides or connects students with tutoring, library support, research help, technology assistance, printing, and study spaces.
- Many Hub services can also be accessed online.
- General Hub phone: (303) 360-4736
- General Hub email: TheHub.cca@ccaurora.edu
- Hub hours: Monday-Thursday, 8 a.m. to 6 p.m.; Friday, 8 a.m. to 5 p.m.; Saturday, 10 a.m. to 2 p.m.

When to use The Hub:
- Use The Hub as a possible starting point for in-person or online campus support.
- For account, login, Wi-Fi, device, printing, or classroom technology problems, use the CCA IT Help Portal or contact the
  CCA IT Helpdesk.
