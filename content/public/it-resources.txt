IT RESOURCES

CCA IT Help Portal:
- The CCA IT Help Portal is a self-service resource for IT-related topics.
- Access it by scanning the IT Help QR code or searching for IT Help on the CCA website.
- The portal provides helpful guidance on common IT issues.

The Hub:
- The Hub may be a starting point for in-person campus support, but current services, locations, phone numbers, and hours
  should be confirmed through official CCA sources before visiting.
- To find current Hub information, search "HUB" from the CCA college home page, use official CCA support pages, or ask the
  CCA IT Helpdesk where to go for your issue.

When to use The Hub:
- Use The Hub as a possible starting point for in-person campus support after confirming current services and locations.
- For account, login, Wi-Fi, device, printing, or classroom technology problems, use the CCA IT Help Portal or contact the
  CCA IT Helpdesk.
