STUDENT EMAIL

You are in the right place. These steps can help you find and open your CCA student email.

What this is:
- All students enrolled at the Community College of Aurora are assigned a college email account.
- The student email account is the college’s primary method of communication with students.
- This guide is for first-time access, account setup, and finding your student email address.

What students use it for:
- Receiving official college communications.
- Getting information about:
  - Scholarships
  - Registration dates
  - Financial deadlines
  - Important academic and administrative notices

What this helps with:
- Use this guide if you need to open student email for the first time.
- Use this guide if you need to find your student email address.
- Use this guide if you are trying to understand which password to use for student email.

Try this first:
1. Open a web browser.
2. Navigate to the Community College of Aurora website.
3. Click the MyCCA link.
4. Log in using the following credentials:
  - Username: Student ID number (S00000000)
  - Password: StudentMMDDYYYY (default) or an updated password created by the student
5. After logging into MyCCA, click the email icon in the top right corner.
6. Enter your CCA email address, which ends in @student.cccs.edu.
7. When prompted for a password, enter your MyCCA password.

MyCCA login:
1. Navigate to the Community College of Aurora website using a web browser.
2. Click the MyCCA link.
3. Log in using your student ID number and password.
4. After the first login, students are required to change their password.

Finding your student email address:
1. Log in to MyCCA.
2. Locate the “Student Email Notification” section in MyCCA.
3. Click the link that says “what is my school email address.”
4. Use the email address shown there when signing in to student email.

Accessing student email:
1. After logging into MyCCA, click the email icon in the top right corner.
2. Enter your CCA email address, which ends in @student.cccs.edu.
3. When prompted for a password, enter your MyCCA password.

Password requirements:
- Passwords must be at least 14 characters long.
- Passwords must include a mix of:
  - Uppercase letters
  - Lowercase letters
  - Numbers and/or special characters

Alternative access:
1. You can also access email by navigating directly to Microsoft Office.
2. Click Sign in and enter your CCA email address and MyCCA password.
3. Launch the Outlook app to view your email.

First access to student email:
- Upon first login, students will be prompted to set up Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA).
- MFA is required to protect Microsoft 365 sign-ins.
- The phone number method is recommended for MFA setup.

On-campus computer login:
- Use the same credentials as MyCCA:
  - Username: Student ID number (S#)
  - Password: StudentMMDDYYYY or updated password
- Updated passwords remain valid for 180 days and continue to work across semesters.

If that did not work:
1. Check that you are using your CCA student email address when signing in to email.
2. Check that you are using your current MyCCA password.
3. If the password does not work, use the CCA Login and Password Help guide.
4. If MFA or verification is blocking sign-in, use the MFA and Account Security guide.
5. If Outlook opens in a browser but not in the app, use the Student Email Troubleshooting guide.

Still need help?:
- If you are unable to log into MyCCA, student email, or campus computers, contact the CCA IT Helpdesk.

What to include when asking for help:
- Your student ID (S#) or CCA username.
- Whether you can sign in to MyCCA.
- Whether you know your CCA student email address.
- Whether the issue happens in Outlook on the web, the Outlook app, or both.
- The exact error message or sign-in prompt you see.
- Whether MFA or verification appears during sign-in.
- What steps you already tried.
