ONLINE AND BLENDED LEARNING (OBL)

What this is:
- The Online and Blended Learning (OBL) team at the Community College of Aurora supports online and blended instruction.
- OBL provides guidance, training, and tools related to learning technologies, course design, accessibility, and quality standards.

Location:
- Fine Arts Building, Room F103

What students use it for:
- Participating in online and blended courses at CCA.
- Accessing course materials and activities supported by learning technologies such as D2L (MyCourses) and Zoom.
- Engaging with accessible and high-quality online course content.

Services provided:

Learning technology support:
- D2L (Learning Management System)
- Zoom (web conferencing)
- YuJa (video creation, storage, and curation)
- Simple Syllabus
- Turnitin (plagiarism checking)
- Video recording room

Quality assurance support:
- 15-week course template
- Healthy Course Checklist
- Design Quality Matters (QM) rubric

Access, equity, and advocacy support:
- D2L accessibility checker
- Equatio (digital math support)
- Read&Write (literacy support tool)
- Accessible Office templates
- Panorama and accessibility training

Training requirements:
- All new faculty and instructors must complete required training before teaching at CCA.
- Required training includes:
  - CCA MyCourses (D2L/Panorama) training
  - Foundations of Online Teaching and Learning (20 hours) for online instructors
- Completion of training is required for future course assignments and is tracked for professional development and pay level advancement.
- Exceptions are handled case by case with Department Chair or Dean approval.

Design and support access:
- Faculty and instructors can book meetings with the OBL design team.
- Technical and instructional support is available via email.

Where to get help / When to contact OBL or IT:
- For online learning design support or instructional questions, contact:
  onlinelearning.cca@ccaurora.edu
- For technical issues with systems such as D2L, Zoom, or login access, contact the IT Helpdesk at 303-360-4900.
