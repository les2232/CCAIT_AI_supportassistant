D2L TROUBLESHOOTING

What this helps with:
- Use this guide if you are having trouble with D2L (Brightspace) at the Community College of Aurora.
- It covers common problems such as not being able to sign in, missing courses, and assignment upload issues.

Cannot access D2L or log into D2L:
- Make sure you are using your correct CCA student account information.
- Start the sign-in process again and make sure your username and password are entered correctly.
- If your login still does not work, the problem may be related to your account, password, or verification method.
- If you cannot access D2L because of a login problem, contact CCA IT Support.

Course not showing:
- If one of your classes is not showing in D2L, first check whether you are looking in the correct term or course area.
- Some courses may not appear until the instructor makes them available.
- If you are enrolled in the course but do not see it, contact your instructor first.

Assignment upload problems:
- Make sure you selected the correct file and that the upload had enough time to finish.
- If the upload does not go through, refresh the page and upload the file again.
- If you are still unable to submit the assignment, contact your instructor right away so they know you are having trouble.

Browser or cache issues:
- If D2L pages are not loading correctly, close the browser and try again.
- You can also try a different browser.
- If the problem continues, clear your browser cache and reopen D2L.

When to contact your instructor:
- Contact your instructor if a course is missing, an assignment is unavailable, a due date looks wrong, or course content does not appear as expected.

When to contact IT:
- Contact CCA IT Support if you cannot sign in to D2L, your account access is not working, or you continue having technical problems after restarting your browser or trying a different browser.
