STUDENT EMAIL

What this is:
- All students enrolled at the Community College of Aurora are assigned a college email account.
- The student email account is the college’s primary method of communication with students.

What students use it for:
- Receiving official college communications.
- Getting information about:
  - Scholarships
  - Registration dates
  - Financial deadlines
  - Important academic and administrative notices

Accessing MyCCA and Student Email:

MyCCA login:
- Navigate to the Community College of Aurora website using a web browser.
- Click the MyCCA link.
- Log in using the following credentials:
  - Username: Student ID number (S00000000)
  - Password: StudentMMDDYYYY (default) or an updated password created by the student
- After the first login, students are required to change their password.

Password requirements:
- Passwords must be at least 14 characters long.
- Passwords must include a mix of:
  - Uppercase letters
  - Lowercase letters
  - Numbers and/or special characters

Accessing student email:
- After logging into MyCCA, click the student email icon in the top right corner.
- Enter your student email address, which ends in @student.cccs.edu.
- If unsure of your email address:
  - Locate the “Student Email Notification” section in MyCCA.
  - Click the link that says “what is my school email address.”
- When prompted for a password, enter your MyCCA password.

Alternative access:
- Student email can also be accessed by navigating directly to Microsoft Office.
- Click Sign in and enter your student email address and MyCCA password.
- Launch the Outlook app to view your email.

First access to student email:
- Upon first login, students will be prompted to set up Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA).
- MFA is required to protect Microsoft 365 sign-ins.
- The phone number method is recommended for MFA setup.

On-campus computer login:
- Use the same credentials as MyCCA:
  - Username: Student ID number (S#)
  - Password: StudentMMDDYYYY or updated password
- Updated passwords remain valid for 180 days and continue to work across semesters.

Where to get help / When to contact IT:
- If you are unable to log into MyCCA, student email, or campus computers, contact:
  - CCA IT Helpdesk: 303-360-4900
  - 24/7 Helpdesk Support: 888-800-9198
