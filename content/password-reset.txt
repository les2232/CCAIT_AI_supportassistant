PASSWORD RESET

What this helps with:
- Use this guide if you need to reset your MyCCA password and get back into your student account.
- Your MyCCA password may also be used for other CCA services such as student email and some campus computers.

When to use it:
- You forgot your password.
- Your password is no longer working.
- Your account is locked.
- You cannot finish sign-in because of a verification or MFA problem.

How to reset your password:
1. Go to the CCA password reset page and follow the prompts.
2. Enter your student account information when asked.
3. Complete the identity verification step.
4. Create a new password and confirm it.
5. Sign in again with your new password in MyCCA and other CCA systems that use the same credentials.

If your account is locked:
- Account lockouts are usually caused by too many incorrect password attempts.
- Wait a few minutes, then try signing in again carefully.
- If you still cannot access your account, contact CCA IT Support for help unlocking it.

MFA or verification problems:
- If you are not getting a verification prompt or code, try the sign-in process again and make sure you are using the correct account information.
- If you changed phones, lost access to your MFA method, or cannot complete verification, CCA IT Support can help.
- MFA problems may also prevent access to student email and other Microsoft 365 services.

If you're still stuck:
- CCA IT Support can help if the password reset process does not work, your account stays locked, or MFA verification keeps failing.
