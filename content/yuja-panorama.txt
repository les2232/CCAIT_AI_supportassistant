YUJA PANORAMA

What this is:
- YuJa Panorama is an accessibility and media support platform used at the Community College of Aurora.
- YuJa helps support course videos, accessibility features, and media content used in CCA courses.

What students use it for:
- Viewing course videos and recorded content provided by instructors.
- Accessing accessible media content and captions.
- Engaging with video materials used in online, blended, and in-person classes.

Student support and access:
- YuJa student resources are available directly through course content when instructors use YuJa-supported videos.
- YuJa integrates with learning platforms used at CCA to support accessible course video access.
- If you cannot access YuJa videos in your course, start from the course content link provided by your instructor and try again.
- If a course video will not open, use the course link again and confirm you are opening the YuJa video from the class content area.

Faculty and instructor context (informational):
- Faculty may also use YuJa for media creation, storage, and accessibility review.
- This section is included for context, but most student support questions are about accessing course videos.
- Faculty training content is informational and separate from the basic student access path.

Video resources available:
- Overview videos introduce YuJa functionality and accessibility tools.
- Additional video resources explain media management and accessibility features.
- These resources are optional and are not required for basic student access to course videos.

Things to know:
- Students typically interact with YuJa through course materials rather than directly managing content.
- Accessibility features are built into YuJa-supported content to improve student access.

Where to get help / When to contact support:
- For accessibility issues related to course videos or YuJa content, students should follow course guidance or contact IT support if the video issue continues.
- For general technical issues, contact the IT Helpdesk at 303-360-4900.
